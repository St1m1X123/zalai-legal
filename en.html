Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026 · Українська версія

This policy describes what personal data ZalAI (the "App", "we") collects, how it is used, and what rights you (the "user") have.

1. What data we collect

Account data

  • Email address
  • Name and profile photo (if you sign in with Google)

Fitness profile

  • Age, gender, height, weight
  • Fitness goal, experience level, training location
  • Available equipment, workouts per week, health notes

Health notes are a special category of data. The field about injuries and limitations is voluntary and optional. By entering this information, you give your explicit consent to its processing for a single purpose — adapting workout recommendations to your limitations. You can change or delete this data at any time in the "Profile" section, or together with your account.

Workout data

  • Lists of exercises, sets, reps and weights
  • Workout dates and duration
  • AI-generated programs and workouts

Technical data

  • AI request log (a counter for rate limiting — 5 generations per day)
  • Crash and error reports (anonymized via Sentry)

2. How we use data

  • Providing personalized workouts and AI recommendations
  • Displaying your progress and statistics
  • Abuse protection (rate limiting of AI generations)
  • Improving stability and fixing errors

We do not sell your data to third parties and do not use it for targeted advertising.

3. Third parties

Supabase — database, authentication and file storage. Servers are located in the EU/US.
supabase.com/privacy

Google (OAuth and Gemini AI) — authentication via your Google account; AI workout generation via the Gemini API. Only the fitness profile and workout parameters required for generation are sent to Gemini — never your name, email or photo.
policies.google.com/privacy

Sentry — automatic crash reports (without personal data).
sentry.io/privacy

4. Storage and security

All data is stored on Supabase servers and protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — each user can only access their own data. Data in transit is protected with HTTPS/TLS.

5. Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → "Delete account"
  • Email: zalai.ua21@gmail.com

Once the request is confirmed, all your data (profile, workouts, programs, avatar) is deleted within 24 hours and cannot be restored.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR and applicable law you have the right to:

  • Receive a copy of your data
  • Correct inaccurate data (via the "Profile" section in the app)
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority

For any requests, contact: zalai.ua21@gmail.com

7. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to this policy

In case of significant changes we will notify you via push notification or update the revision date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
